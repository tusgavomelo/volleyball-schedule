GAME TIMES: The game time listed each week is the time games actually start. Teams are expected to arrive 10 minutes prior to the listed time to stretch and prepare.

SCHEDULE CHANGES: There may be schedule changes throughout the season due to facility availability and cancellations. Players should always check this page prior to leaving for their game each week.

TEAM OF THE WEEK: If you played a team with outstanding sportsmanship in any of your games, make sure you nominate them for the Integrity Cup. Each week, a nominee will be drawn and become our Canadian Brewhouse Team of the Week. Nominate a team here.

MNP CENTRE: An access card will be required to enter MNP Centre. Passes will be sent out to all players prior to the first game.
